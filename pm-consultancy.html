Engagement Models

Structured to suit you

We don’t do one-size-fits-all. Every engagement is structured around what your project actually needs.

📋
Per-Project

Fixed scope, fixed fee. Clear deliverables, defined start and end. Ideal for discrete projects where you know what you need.

🔄
Retained

A set number of days per month over an agreed period. Consistent support for ongoing programmes or growing businesses.

⚡
Advisory

Ad hoc expert input when you need a second opinion, a review or specialist advice without a full engagement.

Consultancy

Specialist expertise where
it matters most

Specialist consultancy across the disciplines that have the greatest impact on construction programmes — contracts, compliance and planning.

📄
NEC Contract Administration

NEC3 and NEC4 contract administration, including Early Warning Notice management, Compensation Event assessment and PM/contractor support throughout the contract cycle. Clause 60.1 entitlement analysis and quotation review.

🦺
CDM 2015 Compliance

Principal Designer services, CDM coordination, pre-construction information packs, Construction Phase Plan review and acceptance, F10 notification, and ongoing site compliance monitoring.

🚧
NRSWA & Streetworks

New Roads and Street Works Act compliance, Section 50 licence coordination, Notice management, Traffic Management planning and Permit scheme support for highway and utility projects.

🏗️
Planning Applications

Planning application support and coordination, pre-application engagement, condition management and discharge, Listed Building and Conservation Area experience, and liaison with local planning authorities.

⚖️
Contract Disputes & Avoidance

Early identification of contractual risk, early warning management, Compensation Event entitlement advice, and support in dispute resolution proceedings.

📊
Project Reviews & Audits

Independent project health checks, programme reviews, risk register audits and procurement assessments. An experienced second pair of eyes at any stage of your project.
