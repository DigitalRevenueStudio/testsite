Matchmaking Power Reduction
Saturday – Sunday UTC coordination message
⚔️ SvS Matchmaking Window 📅 Sat & Sun UTC only 🔹 Reduce state power: ✅ Remove ALL hero gear ✅ Drill Camp reset heroes 🆗 Fill infirmaries 🆗 Burn inactive troops 🔹 Coordinate top 100-150: ✅ All act SAME TIME ✅ Re-equip after Sunday
Tip: Pin this in alliance chat before the matchmaking window opens. Remind members the evening before to be ready.
⚠ Community consensus only. Developers state power reduction does not affect matchmaking. The community broadly disagrees. The risk of trying is zero. Apply your own judgement before sharing with your alliance.
